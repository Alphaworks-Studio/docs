Getting started takes less than a minute. Create a free account and you can begin uploading and repairing models right away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the app and sign up">
    Go to the 3D Prep Pro app and click **Sign Up**. You can register with your email address, or sign up instantly with Google or Apple.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    If you signed up with email, check your inbox for a verification code. Enter the code to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Land on your dashboard">
    Once verified, you're taken to the home dashboard. You're ready to upload your first model.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Mimaki printer owners** get exclusive pricing at $60/month instead of $90/month. If you own a Mimaki 3D printer, see [Mimaki printer owners](/account/mimaki-owners) to activate your discount during signup.
</Note>

## What you get for free

Every new account includes **2 free repairs** — no credit card required. This lets you try the full repair pipeline before deciding on a subscription.

<Tip>
  After your 2 free repairs, you can start a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. See [Free trial](/account/free-trial) for details.
</Tip>
