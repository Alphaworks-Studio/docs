The home dashboard is the first thing you see after signing in. It's designed to get you from upload to repair as quickly as possible.

## What's on the dashboard

### Upload area
The large upload zone at the top lets you drag and drop files immediately. You can also click **Browse** to pick files from your computer.

### How it works
A visual 4-step guide shows the workflow: **Upload → Analyze → Repair → Export**. This is the core flow you'll follow every time.

### Latest uploads
Your three most recently uploaded models appear at the bottom with 360° thumbnail previews. Click any model card to view its details, or click **Show All** to browse your full library.

## What happens after upload

When you drop a file onto the upload area:
1. The file uploads to our servers (you see a progress bar)
2. We automatically analyze the model — scanning geometry, calculating dimensions and volume
3. 360° thumbnail images are rendered from multiple angles
4. The model card appears in your latest uploads section, ready to repair

<Tip>
  You can upload multiple files at once. Each file is processed independently and appears in your library as it completes.
</Tip>
