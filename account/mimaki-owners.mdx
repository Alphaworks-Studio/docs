If you own a Mimaki 3D printer, you can verify your printer ownership to unlock the desktop fixer engine. Mimaki owners and Standard users both subscribe at **$60/month**.

## How to set up your Mimaki account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up through the Mimaki page">
    Visit the Mimaki signup page. You can sign up with email, Google, or Apple.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your printer ownership">
    After creating your account, open the printer verification page at `/account/printer-verification` (also reachable from your Account Settings) and enter your Mimaki printer's serial number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Mimaki approval">
    A Mimaki admin reviews and approves your submission. While your request is pending, the page shows a "Waiting for Mimaki admin review" status and checks for updates every 10 seconds — you can also select **Check now**. Once approved, the desktop fixer engine is activated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe">
    Proceed to checkout for the $60/month plan. If your account is trial-eligible, a 30-day free trial applies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Serial number format

Your serial number is printed on a label on the back or underside of your Mimaki 3D printer. For example: **3DUJ-553-A12345**.

There's no strict format requirement — enter the serial exactly as printed. It can be up to 64 characters long.

<Note>
  Don't have your serial number handy? You can create your account first and verify your printer ownership later from your Account Settings.
</Note>

## Already signed up without Mimaki verification?

If you signed up through the regular signup flow, your account isn't flagged as a Mimaki signup, so the printer verification page won't be available. Contact Mimaki support to have your account linked as a Mimaki owner.

<Tip>
  The Mimaki plan includes all the same features as the Standard plan at the same $60/month price. As a Mimaki owner, verifying your printer ownership is what unlocks the desktop fixer engine.
</Tip>
