If you own a Mimaki 3D printer, you qualify for a special discounted subscription at **$60/month** instead of the standard $90/month.

## How to activate your Mimaki discount

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up through the Mimaki page">
    Visit the Mimaki signup page. You can sign up with email, Google, or Apple.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your printer serial number">
    After creating your account, you'll be asked for your Mimaki printer serial number. The format is letters followed by a dash and digits — for example, **ABC-1234567**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verification">
    We verify your serial number. If valid, your account is flagged for Mimaki pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe at the discounted rate">
    Proceed to checkout where you'll see the $60/month plan instead of $90/month. The 30-day free trial applies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Serial number format

Your serial number is printed on a label on your Mimaki printer. It follows this format:

- 2–4 letters, followed by a dash, followed by 5–10 digits
- Example: **ABC-1234567** or **MK-12345678**

<Note>
  Don't have your serial number handy? You can skip this step during signup and verify later from your Account Settings.
</Note>

## Already signed up without Mimaki verification?

If you signed up through the regular signup flow, contact support to add your Mimaki serial number and switch to the discounted plan.

<Tip>
  The Mimaki plan includes all the same features as the Standard plan — the only difference is the price.
</Tip>
