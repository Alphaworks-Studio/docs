3D Prep Pro uses cookies to keep you signed in and improve your experience. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time.

## Cookie consent

When you first visit the site, a banner asks you to accept cookies. You can:
- **Accept** — Allow all cookies
- **Settings** — Customize which cookies are allowed

## Changing your preferences

Go to **Account Settings** and scroll to the Cookie Settings section. Toggle the **Allow cookies** switch to change your preference.

<Note>
  Essential cookies (required for sign-in and basic functionality) cannot be disabled.
</Note>
