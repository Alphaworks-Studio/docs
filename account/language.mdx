3D Prep Pro is available in six languages. Switch at any time — the entire interface updates immediately.

## Supported languages

| Language | Native name |
|---|---|
| English | English |
| Japanese | 日本語 |
| German | Deutsch |
| Spanish | Español |
| Portuguese | Português |
| Chinese | 中文 |

## How to switch

Click the **language icon** in the top navigation bar (next to your profile). Select your preferred language from the dropdown. The change applies instantly across the entire app.

<Note>
  Your language preference is saved to your account and persists across devices and sessions.
</Note>
