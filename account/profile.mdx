Access your profile settings by clicking your name in the top-right corner and selecting **Settings**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/account/settings.png" alt="Account settings page" />
</Frame>

## Edit your name

Update your first and last name in the Edit Profile section. Click **Save** to confirm.

## Profile photo

- Click your current photo (or the placeholder) to upload a new image
- Click **Delete** to remove your photo and use the default avatar

## Change email

Changing your email is a three-step verification process:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your new email">
    Type your new email address in the Change Email section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    A verification code is sent to your new email. Enter the code to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set as primary">
    Once verified, the new email becomes your primary email for sign-in and notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Change password

Enter your current password, then your new password twice to confirm. Click **Update Password**.

## Subscription management

The Subscription section shows:
- Your current plan name and status (active, trial, canceled)
- Days remaining in your trial (if applicable)
- Next billing date
- **Manage Subscription** — opens the payment portal to update card details or view invoices
- **Cancel Subscription** — cancels at the end of the current billing period (you keep access until then)
- **Reactivate** — if canceled, reactivate before the period ends to continue without interruption

## Billing history

A table showing all billing events: subscription created, payments, failures, cancellations, and reactivations. Each entry shows the date and amount.

## Delete account

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your models, repairs, and settings are permanently deleted.
</Warning>

To delete your account, scroll to the bottom of the settings page and click **Delete Account**. You'll be asked to type "Delete" to confirm.
