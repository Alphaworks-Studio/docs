## Free tier

Every account gets **2 free repairs** — no credit card required. This lets you try the full repair pipeline before committing to a subscription.

## Subscription plans

| | **Standard** | **Mimaki Owner** |
|---|---|---|
| **Price** | $90/month | $60/month |
| **Unlimited repairs** | Yes | Yes |
| **All 19 editor tools** | Yes | Yes |
| **AI Assistant** | Yes | Yes |
| **All export formats** | Yes | Yes |
| **Priority processing** | Yes | Yes |
| **Eligibility** | Everyone | Mimaki printer owners with valid serial |

Both plans include a **30-day free trial** with full access to all features.

<Tip>
  Own a Mimaki 3D printer? You qualify for the discounted Mimaki Owner plan at $60/month. See [Mimaki printer owners](/account/mimaki-owners) for details.
</Tip>

## What's included

Every paid subscription gives you:
- **Unlimited model repairs** with all quality levels
- **All 19 editor tools** — cut, lattice, label, mirror, measure, and more
- **AI Assistant** — chat-based guidance and automated operations
- **All export formats** — OBJ, STL, GLB, 3MF
- **Priority processing** — faster repair times
- **All supported import formats** — including DICOM and LAS

## After your free repairs

Once you've used your 2 free repairs, you'll be prompted to start a subscription. Your uploaded models and repair history are preserved — nothing is deleted.

<Note>
  You can manage your subscription, update payment, and view billing history in [Account Settings](/account/profile).
</Note>
