## Email support

Send us an email at **support@3dprepro.com**. We typically respond within 24 hours on business days.

## What to include

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

- **Your account email** — so we can look up your account
- **What you were trying to do** — upload, repair, use a tool, etc.
- **What happened** — error messages, unexpected behavior, or screenshots
- **Your model details** — file format, polygon count, and file size (if relevant)
- **Your browser and device** — Chrome/Safari/Edge, Windows/Mac/mobile
