The 3D editor is where you inspect, modify, and prepare your model for printing. It combines a powerful 3D viewer with 19 tools and an AI assistant.

## Layout

- **Header bar** — Model name, back button, and file info
- **3D viewport** — The main area where your model is displayed. Rotate, pan, and zoom to inspect from every angle.
- **Toolbar** — Right side, organized into three groups: [Pipeline Tools](/editor/toolbar-layout), [Interactive Tools](/editor/toolbar-layout), and [Inspection Tools](/editor/toolbar-layout)
- **Settings panel** — Opens on the right when you select a tool. Configure options here.
- **AI Assistant** — Click the sparkle icon at the bottom right to open the [AI chat panel](/editor/ai-assistant/overview)

## How to open the editor

Click **View in 3D** from any model detail page or repair detail page. The editor loads your model in the viewport.

## Working with tools

1. Click a **tool icon** in the toolbar — its settings panel opens on the right
2. **Adjust settings** using the sliders, inputs, and toggles
3. **Apply** the tool (for interactive tools) or the settings carry over to the next repair (for pipeline tools)

The active tool icon highlights blue. A small blue dot on any tool icon means you've customized its settings from the defaults.

<Note>
  Some interactive tools require a **repaired** (print-ready) model. These tools appear dimmed if your model hasn't been repaired yet.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Not sure where to start? Open the [AI Assistant](/editor/ai-assistant/overview) and ask "What should I do to prepare this for printing?" — it will analyze your model and give specific recommendations.
</Tip>
