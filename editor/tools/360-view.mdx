360° View shows your model's pre-rendered thumbnails from multiple angles and lets you customize viewport lighting and background for better visualization.

## Settings

### Lighting preset
- **Studio** — Balanced, professional lighting
- **Natural** — Soft, outdoor-style lighting
- **Dramatic** — High contrast with strong shadows
- **Flat** — Even lighting, shows colors most accurately

### Background theme
- **Dark** — Dark background (default)
- **Light** — White/light background
- **Gradient** — Soft gradient backdrop
- **Transparent** — No background

### Auto-rotate
Toggle automatic rotation with adjustable speed. Great for getting a full view of your model without manual mouse movement.

## Tips

- Use **Dramatic** lighting to highlight surface details and potential printing issues
- Use **Flat** lighting to see colors most accurately — helpful for full-color prints
- Enable **auto-rotate** for a quick overview of the entire model
