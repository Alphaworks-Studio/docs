Wall Thickness sets the minimum thickness any wall in your model can have. During repair, walls thinner than this value are automatically thickened.

## When to use this

When your model has delicate features, thin edges, or fine details that might break during printing or post-processing.

## Settings

### Thickness (0–10 mm)
The minimum wall thickness. **Default: 1.0 mm.**

**Recommended values by printer type:**
| Printer | Minimum thickness |
|---------|------------------|
| FDM | 1.0–1.5 mm |
| SLA / Resin | 0.5–1.0 mm |
| Full-color (Mimaki) | 0.7–1.0 mm |
| SLS | 0.7–1.0 mm |

## Step by step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the Thickness tool">
    Click **Wall Thickness** in the toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your minimum thickness">
    Adjust the value based on your printer's requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a repair">
    This setting takes effect during the next repair.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Setting thickness below **0.5 mm** may result in walls that crumble or break during printing or post-processing.
</Warning>
