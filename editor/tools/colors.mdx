Colors controls how your model's textures and materials are handled during repair.

## When to use this

When your model has multiple material slots or complex texture setups, and you want to control which texture information is preserved.

## Settings

### Texture source
- **Diffuse Only** (default) — Uses only the base color texture. Best for most 3D printing.
- **All Maps** — Includes normal maps, roughness, and other texture channels. Use for advanced full-color printing where you want lighting effects baked in.

<Tip>
  For full-color printing, **Diffuse Only** usually produces the most accurate colors. **All Maps** may add unexpected shading from lighting data baked into other texture channels.
</Tip>
